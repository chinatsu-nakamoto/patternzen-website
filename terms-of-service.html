2. Service Description

2.1 Service Overview

JapanesePatternsZEN is a mobile application that provides traditional Japanese patterns (wagara). The App offers:

  • Pattern Browsing: Digital collection of traditional Japanese patterns
  • High-Resolution Downloads: High-quality images available for commercial use
  • Cultural Information: English explanations of origin, history, and meaning
  • Bookmark Feature: Save favorite patterns
  • Viewing History: Record of recently viewed patterns
  • Subscription Plans: Free and paid plans

2.2 Data Sources

Pattern images are sourced from:

  • National Diet Library (NDL) Image Bank
  • NDL Digital Collections (materials whose copyright protection period has expired)

All pattern images have been confirmed to be in the public domain.
